Welcome to the Rational RequisitePro Tutorial

The Rational RequisitePro Tutorial is an online tutorial designed to introduce you to RequisitePro, Rational's requirements management tool. You will be introduced to key features of RequisitePro that will help you manage projects successfully, and you will have an opportunity to use the software to create a project and to work with an existing one.

Audience

Read this tutorial if you are a member of a development team (an analyst, manager, or developer) and are unfamiliar with the requirements management process and RequisitePro. To get the most out of this tutorial, you should have a basic understanding of the Microsoft Windows operating environment.
